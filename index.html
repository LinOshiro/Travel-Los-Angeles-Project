Why Los Angeles?

The City of Angels

Los Angeles is a city with many names. One of which is simply the city's initials, L.A. It is also called the City of Angels because Los Angeles means ‚Äúthe angels‚Äù in Spanish. Los Angeles is a city with a sunny and warm climate giving it the nickname of City of Flowers and Sunshine.

Los Angeles is a city full of diversity which is another reason to visit. This city is full of many activities, from hikes in the Los Angeles National Forest, to a calming walk on one of many beaches. For a busier atmosphere attend a theme park, such as Universal Studios, or walk the busy streets of the Hollywood Boulevard or Venice Beach. If you are looking for some family friendly fun, head to Santa Monica Pier. There really is something for everyone in LA LA Land.

My Favorite Places

Universal Studios

Family friendly theme park for all ages. Located on the Studio lots of Universal Pictures. Here you can adventure through many lands related to classic movies and shows. Such as Harry Potter, The Simpsons, Minions, and JAWS!

Address:

100 Unniversal City Plaza

What I like about it

Fun location day and night! There is also a city walk outside of Universal full of shopping and restaurants.

Manhattan Beach Pier

A great place for all! Full of waves, sand and lots of sun. A cute little town in the Southbay area of Los Angeles county. Lots of shopping and restaurants to explore after your beach excursion.

Address:

2 Manhattan Beach Blvd

What I like about it

This beach city is full of laid back locals, great outdoor or indoor dining, and a beautiful beach.

The Walk of Fame

A historic landmark with over 2,700 stars embedded along 15 blocks of Hollywood. These are permanent public monuments. Enjoy walking around the sunny streets looking for your favorite entertainers name.

Address:

Hollywoood Blvd/Highland St.

What I like about it

Great location for tourists to get a taste of Hollywood and for hopefuls looking to run into a celebrity.
